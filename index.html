1 Introduction

In this notebook, I will demonstrate several issues of the implementation of the aligned rank transform procedure (Wobbrock et al. 2011). Some of these issues have been raised by other researchers in the past (Barr et al. 2013; Luepsen 2017, 2018) and have not been addressed by the more recent implementation of the procedure (Elkin et al. 2021). I will explain that there are simpler and more general transformation methods that avoid these issues.
